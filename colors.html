When you're feeling angry, it can be helpful to focus on colors that promote relaxation and serenity. Cool and soothing colors are known to have a calming effect on emotions. Some light colors that you might consider are: 1. Sky Blue: This color is often associated with tranquility and can help to create a sense of calmness. 2. Pale Green: Green is known to have a soothing effect on both the mind and body. Pale or mint green shades can be particularly calming. 3. Lavender: This light purple shade is often associated with relaxation and can help to reduce feelings of anger or stress. 4. Soft Gray: Gray is a neutral color that can bring a sense of calmness and balance. Opting for a soft gray can create a soothing atmosphere. If you'd like to combine colors, you could try a combination such as a sky blue and pale green or lavender and soft gray. Experiment and see which combination resonates most with you.
